Santorini, Greece

Duration: 7 Days Season: Summer 2023
White-washed walls against azure skies,
Where ancient tales and beauty lies.
Sunset paints the caldera's rim,
As day fades, growing soft and dim.
Perched on the edge of a volcanic caldera, Santorini is a crescent-shaped wonder where traditional Cycladic architecture meets dramatic cliff-side views. Every corner tells a story of ancient civilization, while every sunset paints a new masterpiece across the Aegean Sea.

Highlights

  • Sunset watching in Oia
  • Wine tasting in traditional vineyards
  • Exploring ancient Akrotiri
  • Swimming in volcanic hot springs
