Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

In tender morsels of golden delight,
Simmered in gravy, rich and bright.
Tomatoes and cream in perfect dance,
A royal feast at first glance.

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken thighs
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • Kashmiri red chili powder

Cooking Instructions

1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 4 hours
2. Grill marinated chicken until charred
3. Prepare the makhani sauce with tomatoes and cream
4. Simmer chicken in the sauce until flavors meld
